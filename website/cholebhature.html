Chole Bhature Recipe

Learn to make authentic and delicious Chole Bhature!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chickpeas (chole), soaked overnight
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • Oil for cooking

For Bhature

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Boil the soaked chickpeas until soft. Set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add onions and fry until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, then add tomato puree, spices, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Add boiled chickpeas, mix well, and simmer for 10-15 minutes. Adjust seasoning as needed.
  5. For Bhature, mix all-purpose flour, baking soda, salt, and yogurt to form a dough. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  6. Roll small portions of dough and deep fry until golden and puffed. Serve hot with chole!

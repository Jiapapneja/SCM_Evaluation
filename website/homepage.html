Suggested Recipes

Chole Bhature

Chole bhature is a food dish popular in the northern areas of the Indian subcontinent. It is a combination of chana masala and bhatura/puri, a deep-fried bread made from maida. Chole bhature is often eaten as a breakfast dish, sometimes accompanied with lassi.

Butter Chicken with Garlic Naan

Garlic Naan with Butter Chicken is a popular Indian dish. Soft, buttery naan flavored with garlic pairs perfectly with the creamy, mildly spiced butter chicken, made from marinated chicken in a rich tomato and cream sauce.

Sushi

Sushi is a Japanese dish made of vinegared rice paired with seafood, vegetables, or fruit. It comes in forms like nigiri (rice with fish), maki (rice and fillings rolled in seaweed), and sashimi (sliced raw fish).

Sushi Recipe

Learn how to make traditional Japanese sushi at home!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sushi rice
  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • 3 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Nori (seaweed sheets)
  • Filling options: fresh salmon, tuna, cucumber, avocado
  • Soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger for serving

Instructions

  1. Rinse the sushi rice until the water runs clear. Combine with water and cook according to package instructions.
  2. In a small bowl, mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Add this mixture to the cooked rice and let it cool.
  3. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat, shiny side down. Spread a thin layer of rice over the nori, leaving 1 inch at the top.
  4. Add your choice of fillings (salmon, tuna, cucumber, avocado) horizontally across the middle of the rice.
  5. Roll the sushi tightly using the bamboo mat, applying gentle pressure. Slice into pieces with a sharp knife.
  6. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger for dipping. Enjoy your homemade sushi!
